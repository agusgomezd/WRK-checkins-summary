Structural themes
Portfolio-wide patterns this week
Aggressive scaling, diminishing returns
5 accounts
House of Leon (+35% spend / conv −27%), Out of Print (+28% / −16%), Tie Boss (+16% / −22%), Simply Good Coffee (+8% / −22%), and POPFLEX (+18% / −6%) pushed budgets but conversions fell and CPA rose.
Disciplined pullback & audits
Portfolio-wide
House of Leon is running a deep scaling audit; Out of Print a granular search-term audit today; Tie Boss is reverting last week's bid scaling; The Luxe Lens is consolidating into proven exact-match ad groups; POPFLEX is refocusing on core remarketing.
The efficient scalers
3 accounts
Baboon held spend flat yet grew conversions 60% at a $13.56 CPA; Ilios doubled spend for a 58% conversion lift; Iron Neck paired a 6% spend rise with a clean 3% conversion gain — the template for how to scale.
Tracking & approval fixes
3 accounts
OmegaVia resolved a geo-filter add-to-cart issue and resumed product-page routing; Lice Doctors restored its v2 landing page after an ad disapproval; Chilly Goat is deferring to Triple Whale through its 23-day platform attribution lag.
